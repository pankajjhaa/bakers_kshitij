Rajpuatana Sanctuary is a non-govermment organisation that is currently active in Gurgoan.
We have started constructing our sanctuary on 15th July 2020, on a rented land in a village near Gurgoan,Haryana.
we are currently working towards a common mission to save and rescue stray animals from road accidents, poisoning case, hunger, and provide them health check-up. our team is working in different parts of Gurgoan to feed animals, we almost feed more than 2000 animals everyday.
we all work on a common vision to create a world where animals are not products, join us now to make our vision come true.
